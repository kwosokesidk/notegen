How It Works

01
Input
Paste text or upload a PDF/TXT file through the clean interface or REST API.
02
Chunk
Long texts are split into 400-word chunks to fit the model's context window.
03
Summarize
DistilBART (Hugging Face) generates a summary for each chunk on CPU.
04
Merge
Chunk summaries are combined and optionally re-summarized into a final result.
